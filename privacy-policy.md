# Privacy Policy for Remarq

Last updated: April 2026

## Overview
Remarq is a Chrome extension that generates AI-powered comments 
for YouTube, LinkedIn, and Substack.

## Data We Collect
We collect only the following:
- Text you highlight on pages (sent to our backend solely to 
  generate a comment, never stored)
- Your preferred comment tone (stored locally in your browser)

## Data We Do NOT Collect
- No personal information
- No browsing history
- No authentication credentials
- No financial information
- No location data

## How Your Data Is Used
Highlighted text is sent to our backend server solely to generate 
a contextually relevant comment using the Anthropic Claude API. 
This data is not logged, stored, or shared with any third party 
beyond Anthropic's API.

## Data Storage
Tone preference is stored locally in your browser using 
chrome.storage.local. No data is stored on our servers.

## Third Parties
We use the Anthropic Claude API solely for comment generation. 
Anthropic's privacy policy applies to data processed by their API.
See: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy
